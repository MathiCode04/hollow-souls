HOLLOW SOULS

A Metroidvania of Shadow and Steel

Controls
Arrow Keys / WASD — Move & Jump
Z / J — Attack (hold for charge)
X / K — Dash (I-frames)
C / L — Focus / Heal (hold, costs soul)
V — Fireball Spell (costs soul)
B — Shockwave Spell (costs soul)
Down + Attack in air — Pogo Bounce
Attack exactly as enemy swings — Parry!
Attack cracked walls to reveal secrets
